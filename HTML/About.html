About Us

Established in 2015, InSIST Net LTD (INET) aims to provide low cost broadband service to homes, SMEs, corporate businesses, government departments and international organizations. We offer 4G LTE services whilst maintaining our legacy WIMAX network with thousands of subscribers. Today, we are the fastest growing ISP and are connecting Gambians to reliable home and office broadband in 80% of the Greater Banjul Area.

We focus on reliability, low cost and high speed internet service with unparalleled customer care, delivering all our services in an efficient and timely manner. We are the first Gambian network provider to guarantee reliability and performance as we own our entire infrastructure.

InSIST NET Ltd is the brainchild of a dynamic and goal oriented Gambian youth, Seedy Omar Bensouda, whose dream is to see that every Gambian has access to reliable and fast internet service at an affordable price. With the vast knowledge and experience shared amongst his team, together they have established and contributed to the development and management of numerous institutions and companies in the market.
